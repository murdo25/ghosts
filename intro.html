The Maze Game

Welcome to our exciting maze game of the chase! Here, two players will duke it out in a battle of supremacy to see who will triumph. Will the ghost make his escape, or will the hunter take him down? Click the link below to begin!

Controls

Ghost

Your purpose is to escape by hitting one of two exits.

  • W - Move Up
  • A - Move Left
  • S - Move Down
  • D - Move Right

Hunter

Your purpose is to capture the ghost by touching him.

  • Up Arrow - Move Up
  • Left Arrow - Move Left
  • Down Arrow - Move Down
  • Right Arrow - Move Right

Eat the hamburger to temporarily increase your speed!
